This is the beginning. A space for ideas that deserve more than a passing thought.

## Why Write in Public?

Writing forces clarity. Half-formed ideas become legible when put into sentences. Connections that live only in intuition gain structure on the page. The act of writing is the act of thinking, not its transcription.

This blog will collect notes on:

- **Research** — papers, methods, open problems
- **Technology** — systems, tools, craft
- **Ideas** — whatever doesn't fit elsewhere

## A Minimal Setup

Built with Jekyll and GitHub Pages. No tracking, no analytics, no comments. Just text, links, and time.

```bash
git clone https://github.com/nobugpal/blog.git
cd blog
bundle install
bundle exec jekyll serve
```

Then visit `http://localhost:4000`.

## On Style

The typography is set in EB Garamond — a typeface with a long history in scholarly publishing. The layout follows the principle of **maximum content, minimum decoration**. What you see is the writing.
